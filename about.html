About

Allen Jiang is an independent developer and computer science student. Having studied at UC Berkeley's Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (EECS) program, he is currently looking to expand his industry experience in software engineering.

As an aspiring software engineer, he is currently creating his own projects to showcase proficiency for actualizing secure and responsive designs with applications for real-world problem-solving. Using both familiar and cutting-edge industry techniques, Allen is creating living solutions ranging from third-party tools for enhancement of massively-multiplayer online video gameplay, to water-quality spectrophotometry for humanitarian and educational applications, to this very website.

Allen is also a full-time instructor and part-time tutor for youth in technology, leveraging his communication skills to spark interest in the next generation of STEM professionals.
